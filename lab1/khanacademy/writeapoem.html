The Sky Is So Blue

I didn't know
that the sky was so blue
We all linked hands and walked out
to look up at the sky together
This perfectly blue sky
The sky taught us
how to be kind
and how to never let go of our friends

I didn't know
that the sky was so blue
Why, I wonder, did I grow sad
When once I looked up at the sky alone
This deeply blue sky
The sky let us listen
to the song of clouds undefeated by the wind
So that we would no longer weep when alone

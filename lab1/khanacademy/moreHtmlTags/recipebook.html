Heavy boiled eggs

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 1-2
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 1-5
Water Water level should be enough to the eggs be completely in

Step 1:Pour water to some capacity
Step 2:Dip eggs completely to the water
Step 3:Wait 7-10 minutes

Medium boiled eggs

  • Time: 7 min
  • Serves: 1-2
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 1-5
Water Water level should be enough to the eggs be more than half but not completely in

Step 1:Pour water to some capacity
Step 2:Dip eggs more than half but not completely to the water
Step 3:Wait 5-7 minutes

Light boiled eggs

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 1-2
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 1-5
Water Water level should be enough to the eggs be half in

Step 1:Pour water to some capacity
Step 2:Dip eggs half to the water
Step 3:Wait 3-5 minutes

Here you go! Your eggs are done!
